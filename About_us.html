About Us

Automag specializes in wholesale and retail delivery of automotive spare parts from Poland to Ukraine. A spare part is a crucial component of your vehicle that requires regular and timely replacement.

When in need of car repairs, individuals often encounter issues related to the availability and supply of auto parts. This can include long waiting times, one-way advance payment, as well as a lack of quality parts and the desired brand.

Automag is a reliable supplier and seller of auto parts. We offer fast delivery times and ensure that our customers receive their products with a guarantee.

Our company has been trading in auto parts since 2003. Our experience in selecting parts, the ability to deliver quickly, and our honesty towards our customers are the three pillars upon which Automag is built.

If necessary, our employees will provide you with guidance on part selection, confirm availability and technical condition, and offer advice on whether a particular item is worth considering or if it's better to choose another. Our company's pricing policy will also pleasantly surprise you.
